Description:

GET/find

Access: RESTRICTED by authentication of the app
Return: [JSON] - list of venues objects
Parameters:
  • ll: String with latitude and longitude of geolocation of user "ll=10.43223,-74.121222"
  • radius: Limit results to venues within this many meters of the specified location
  • public_key: Public key of the app
  • auth_code: Code generated with private key and modifiers
  • valide_date: [Optional] Specifies whether or not to consider the date to generate the auth_code "aaaa/mm/dd hh"
  • modifiers: Modifiers used for encode the private key of the app "modifiers=m1,m2,...mn"
